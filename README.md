## Dev Tools 🧙‍♀️

* [WAGMI](https://wagmi.sh/) - React Hooks library for Ethereum, built on ethers.js.
* [HardHat](https://hardhat.org/) - Ethereum development environment for professionals.
* [Foundry](https://github.com/foundry-rs/foundry) - Blazing fast Solidity toolkit with forge, cast, and anvil for testing, deploying, and interacting with smart contracts.
* [Openzeppelin](https://openzeppelin.com/) - The standard for secure blockchain applications.
* [Viem](https://viem.sh/) - TypeScript interface for Ethereum, providing lightweight and type-safe modules for interacting with the blockchain.
* [Radicle](https://radicle.xyz/) - Easily share your code without relying on a third party.
* [Rainbow Kit](https://www.rainbowkit.com/docs/introduction) - RainbowKit is a React library that makes it easy to add wallet connection to your dapp. It's intuitive, responsive and customizable.
* [Thirdweb](https://thirdweb.com/) - Full-stack web3 development platform with SDKs, pre-built contracts, and infrastructure services.
* [The Graph](https://thegraph.com/) - Indexing protocol for querying blockchain data with GraphQL, powering many DeFi and DAO dashboards.
* [Alchemy](https://www.alchemy.com/) - Blockchain development platform with enhanced APIs, webhooks, and debugging tools.


## Governance Tools 🧑‍⚖️

* [Aragon](https://aragon.org/) - Build your Decentralized Autonomous Organization on open-source infrastructure with governance plugins.
* [Snapshot](https://snapshot.org) - Snapshot is a decentralized voting system. It provides flexibility on how voting power is calculated for a vote.
* [Tally](https://www.tally.xyz/) - Tally's no-code tool submits DAO proposals for a vote. For example, send funds from the treasury or call a smart contract.
 by creating tools around a DAO's most basic primitive: people.
* [Syncra](https://www.syncra.xyz/) - A modular DAO management platform, focuses on privacy through implementation of anonymous voting.
* [DAOhaus](https://daohaus.club/) - No-code platform for launching and managing Moloch-style DAOs with built-in proposal and voting workflows.
* [Colony](https://colony.io/) - DAO framework with reputation-weighted governance, task management, and budget allocation.
* [Karma](https://www.karmahq.xyz/) - Data-driven tools to measure contributions and strengthen DAO governance with delegation dashboards and contributor tracking.
* [Kryon](https://kryon.fit/) - AI-powered governance infrastructure that automates proposal summarization, risk evaluation, and voting with auditable records.
* [MarketDAO](https://marketdao.dev/) - Market-driven governance with tradable voting tokens, enabling dynamic markets around governance decisions.

## Organization Tools 🧠

* [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) - Notion is a software designed to help members of a company or organization coordinate deadlines, objectives, and assignments for the sake of efficiency and productivity.


* [Charmverse](https://charmverse.io/) - Web3-native workspace for proposals, bounties, member directories, and token-gated content.

## Treasury Management 💰

* [Gnosis Safe](https://gnosis-safe.io/) - The most trusted platform to manage digital assets on Ethereum.
* [Coinshift](https://coinshift.xyz/) - Treasury management with multi-sig support, batch payments, and real-time reporting.
* [Coincile](https://coincile.io/) - Unified treasury dashboards, multi-sig wallet tracking, grant management, and audit trails.
* [Superfluid](https://www.superfluid.finance/) - Programmable money streams for real-time salary, subscriptions, and continuous token distributions.
* [JuiceBox](https://juicebox.money/) - Community-owned treasury and funding platform for programmable spending.

## Contributor Compensation 💸

* [Coordinape](https://coordinape.com/) - Decentralized compensation through peer-allocated GIVE tokens, removing the need for a central payroll authority.

## Legal & Compliance ⚖️

* [Kali](https://docs.kali.gg/) - End-to-end DAO launch platform with instant Delaware LLC legal wrapper formation and liability protection.
* [DAObox](https://daobox.io/) - Legal wrapper management including directors, corporate accounting, anonymity protection, and DAO reporting.
* [Otoco](https://otoco.io/) - On-chain entity formation to spin up a real-world LLC directly from a smart contract.
* [DAOlegals](https://www.daolegals.io/) - Automated incorporation with jurisdiction matching across Web3-friendly countries like Wyoming, Cayman Islands, and Switzerland.

## Identity & Reputation 🪪

* [Galxe](https://galxe.com/) - Web3 credential platform for building on-chain reputation, loyalty programs, and community engagement.
* [Gitcoin Passport](https://passport.gitcoin.co/) - Sybil-resistance identity verification aggregating web2 and web3 credentials into a single score.
* [Guild.xyz](https://guild.xyz/) - Token-gated access management for communities across Discord, Telegram, GitHub, and more.

## Analytics & Data 📊

* [Boardroom](https://boardroom.io/) - Governance aggregator with unified proposal tracking and voting across multiple DAOs.
